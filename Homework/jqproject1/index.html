How to Make A Paper Airplane

Below are the materials, steps, and photos on how to make a (very basic) paper airplane.

Materials

  1. Hands
  2. A Piece of Paper
  3. Basic Folding Skills

Steps

First

Fold the paper in half (hot dog style).

Second

Unfold your paper.

Third

Fold two corners of the paper into the center of the paper.

Fourth

Fold the paper in half again (still hot dog style).

Fifth

Fold the top half down on both sides of the paper.
